Our Story

In the heart of Invercargill, a small slice of the Philippines has found its home. Our lodge and grocery store is a labor of love, a place where the warmth of Filipino culture mingles with the rich flavors of our homeland. We invite you to be a part of our journey, where we've been sharing a taste of the Philippines with our community for several years now.

A Growing Legacy

Over the years, our lodge and grocery store have grown and evolved, just like the community we serve. We've become a beloved gathering place for Filipinos, both as a place to stock up on familiar ingredients and as a hub for connecting with friends and neighbors who share a love for Filipino culture. At the heart of our story is our commitment to you, our valued customers and guests. We're not just a business; we're a part of your lives, sharing in your experiences, and being here to support you in your journey. As we look back on our time in Invercargill, we're excited for the future and the opportunities it holds to continue serving our community with the flavors of the Philippines and the warmth of Filipino hospitality. Thank you for being a part of our story, and we can't wait to see what the next chapter will bring. Mabuhay!
